I am currently a post-doctoral researcher in the DataShape.

I am working on interactions between Topological data analysis (TDA) and Optimal transport (OT). I am interested in both theoretical and computational aspects of these two fields, targetting applications in statistics and maching learning. More recently, I got interested in continuous model in machine learning, for instance neural ODEs.
