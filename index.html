I am currently maître de conférence (~ lecturer/associate prof) in the Laboratoire d'Informatique Gaspard Monge, Université Gustave Eiffel.

I am working on interactions between Topological data analysis (TDA) and Optimal transport (OT). I am interested in both theoretical and computational aspects of these two fields, targeting applications in statistics and maching learning. More recently, I got interested in continuous model in machine learning, for instance neural ODEs.
